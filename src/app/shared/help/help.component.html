Thanks for using Fakebook Ninja!

How to login:

Logging in is easy.  On the /auth page, just click the Switch To Signup  button and enter your email and the password you want to use.   It can be any email address you want, even a fake one.   The songs you create in this app will be owned by that email address.

After signing up, you will be automatically logged in.

How to use the editor:

Click the New Song button create a new song. Remember to use the Manage menu to fetch your existing songs first, or else you might lose your work.   The editor will only allow you to edit songs that you own.

When editing a new song, there will be some default values.

Every song has data and meta .

Here is what data might look like:

[
  ["C6"],["C6"],["C6"],["Gm7","C7"],["F7"],["F7"],["C6"],["C/E","Eb°"],
  ["Dm7"],["G7"],["C/E","Eb°"],["D-7","G7"]]

Here is what meta might look like. It is your responsibility to experiment with these values to get your chart to look like you want it to.

  {{ data | json }}

After editing your song, always use the Manage menu to store your work and then double check it to be sure it saved.

Some chord symbols you might need can be copied from here: ♭ ø7 o ° M11 Δ

Share songs with others:

Users who are not logged in can see charts if you send them a link to the chart.

The public link to charts looks like this, with /public on the end of it: http://localhost:4200/songs/2/public

Save this link into your bookmarks and share it with your friends. It is all FREE!
Enjoy!
